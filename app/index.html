Welcome to Premier Endodontic Associates

Thank you for your interest in Premier Endodontic Associates Inc., the offices of Drs. Brian Wardell, Bruna Burgener and Patrick O'Hara. In our office, we are dedicated exclusively to all aspects of endodontic care.
Endodontics, or "root canal therapy", is one of the specialties currently recognized by the American Dental Association. Our practices in Hinsdale and Wheaton have been serving the needs of the Western suburbs for over 15 years.
